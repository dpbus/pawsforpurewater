Water... the most basic of life's needs, is unavailable to thousands of people in many parts of the world. The only water they might have access to could be miles away, and is often diseased and bacteria ridden.

A well can change the vitality of a community for generations to come. It is a first step towards transforming, and often saving, many lives. This is our mission. We are dedicated to raising as much as we can, using 100 percent of our profits, and finding the most competent and economical resources to build clean water wells in deeply impoverished areas of the world. We are currently in the planning process for our first well in Africa.
