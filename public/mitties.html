Our Mitties

All of our Mitties are made with the same felted wool and loving care as our mittens. Every pair is unique and special. They are the perfect hand-made gift for teachers,mail carriers, hairdressers, hostesses, etc. They hold gift cards, decorate packages, wine bottles, doorknobs, trees, etc.

Like our mittens, all profits go directly towards building wells that provide clean drinking water in impoverished areas. A gift that gives back!
