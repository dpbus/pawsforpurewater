Our Mittens

All of our mittens are hand made using recycled wool or cashmere sweaters that have been donated or purchased at resale stores.

These are just a few samples of our beautiful mittens, but we have many more to choose from. Each pair is unique and special...and warm! The perfect gift! We fill custom orders when possible, sometimes using sweaters that have special sentimental value. We also have a talent among us whose embellished mittens are works of art.

We're always working on new products to add to our line. We'll keep you posted. Go to Contact Us to set up a mitten showing or party.

Most of our mittens are $30, but prices vary on our super special ones.
